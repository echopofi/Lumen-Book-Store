Build output — route table excerpt:
  ○ /contact

  /contact is marked ○ (Static) — client component, no dynamic server data.

How to test:
  1. Visit http://localhost:3000/contact.
  2. Fill in name, email, and message.
  3. Submit — on success, a green confirmation appears with "Send another
     message" button that shows the form again; on failure, a red error
     message is displayed.
  4. If Gmail blocks the connection (common with nodemailer + Gmail):
     - The SMTP_PASSWORD must be a Gmail App Password (16-letter code), not
       the regular account password. App Passwords require 2FA enabled on the
       Google account.
     - Less secure app access is no longer supported by Google — App Passwords
       are the only way.
     - If "blocked by Google" errors occur, the account owner should visit
       https://myaccount.google.com/security and check for "Less secure app
       access" or generate an App Password at
       https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords.
      - Gmail's rate limits (500 emails/day for personal accounts) may also
        cause failures under heavy use.
